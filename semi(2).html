Welcome to SHEvolve

Empowering women in tech through career development and community

Why SHEvolve Exists

Bridging the Gender Gap

SHEvolve was created to address the significant underrepresentation of women in tech and leadership roles. Only 26% of computing jobs are held by women, and we're here to change that.

Our Mission

We provide women with the tools, resources, and community support needed to:

  • Develop in-demand technical skills
  • Navigate career advancement challenges
  • Build confidence in male-dominated fields
  • Connect with mentors and peers
